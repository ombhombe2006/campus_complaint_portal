Common Questions

Everything you need to know about reporting campus issues

How fast do you respond to reports?

Emergency issues are addressed within 2-4 hours. Standard issues typically get resolved within 24-48 hours. You'll receive updates throughout the process.

Can I track my report status?

Absolutely! Every report gets a unique tracking number. Log into your dashboard to see real-time updates, photos from our team, and estimated completion times.

What details should I include in my report?

Include the exact location (building, floor, room), detailed description, photos if possible, and urgency level. More details help our team respond faster and more effectively.

Who can use this system?

All enrolled students, faculty, and staff can use CampusFix. Simply register with your college email address and student/employee ID to get started.

What if it's an emergency?

For immediate safety hazards, call campus security at (555) 123-4567 first. Then file a report through our system for documentation and follow-up.

Is there a mobile app?

Our website is fully mobile-optimized and works great on all devices. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon with additional features like photo uploads and push notifications.
